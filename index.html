About Startup Expo 2025

Organised by the E-Cell at MIT World Peace University, Startup Expo 2025 is a pioneering platform bringing together over 60 startups daily, more than 20 investors, and thousands of students, corporates, and enthusiasts to celebrate entrepreneurship and innovation.

From product showcases and live pitches to mentorship and networking, Startup Expo is your gateway to connect with the brightest minds shaping tomorrow’s world.

About MIT-WPU

MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU) stands as a beacon of transformative education rooted in peace and innovation. With over 65,000 students across disciplines such as engineering, medicine, pharmacy, management, and design, MIT-WPU is committed to holistic development, practical learning, and fostering global impact.

About Wadhwani Foundation

The Wadhwani Foundation is a global non-profit organization dedicated to accelerating economic development in emerging economies through entrepreneurship, innovation, and job creation. Founded by Dr. Romesh Wadhwani, the foundation focuses on empowering entrepreneurs and fostering innovation to create sustainable economic growth.

FAQ

Q) What happens in the event??

Startup Expo 2025 is a major event that typically features a variety of activities aimed at fostering entrepreneurship, leadership and networking among startups, investors, and industry professionals.

Q) When is the Event for this year??

The Startup Expo 2025 event is scheduled to take place from August 11th to August 14th.

Q) What do the attendees get??

Attendees of Startup Expo 2025 gain access to valuable networking opportunities, insights from industry experts, exposure to innovative products and services, potential investment opportunities, and the chance to participate in workshops, panel discussions, and pitch sessions.

Q) Is there any Registration Fees??

No, there is no registration fee for attending Startup Expo 2025.
